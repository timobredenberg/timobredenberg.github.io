Timo Bredenberg

Bio

Timo Bredenberg is an artist living and working in Tampere, Finland.

In his works, including moving image, installation, software and text-based art, Bredenberg has examined network capitalism, digitalization, financialization and their manifestation in the built environment and language. Bredenberg is currently interested in machine learning and history of natural language processing and their effect on the archival of human knowledge. Developing artistic methodologies which utilize new technologies is central to his practice.
