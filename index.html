Timo Bredenberg

Bio

Timo Bredenberg is an artist living and working in Tampere, Finland.

In his works, including moving image, installation, software and text-based art, Bredenberg has examined network capitalism, digitalization, financialization and their manifestation in the built environment and language. Bredenberg is currently interested in machine learning, natural language processing and their effect on the transmission and archival of human knowledge. Developing art methodologies which utilize technology in non-conventional ways is central to his practice.
