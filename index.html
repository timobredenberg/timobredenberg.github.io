Timo Bredenberg

Bio

Visual artist Timo Bredenberg works and lives in Tampere, Finland. In his work he has explored internet communities and movements using methods from found footage cut-up to virtual derivé. Recently he has been interested in network economies, their material pre-conditions and underlying algorithms.
